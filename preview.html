FAQ: TEXT STYLES

What is a font?

A font is a collection of printable and displayable text characters that determine how your sign looks. There are two main types of fonts: Serif and san-serif.

What is a serif font?

Serifs are the tail-looking small lines attached to the end of a stroke in a letter or symbol. Serif fonts can deliver a classic and prestigeous look to your text.

What is a sans-serif font?

As the French prefix implies, sans-serif means "without serif". Sans-serif fonts are typically used for modern and sharp designs.

What is text alignment?

Text alignment is the arrangement of your text relative to its margin. We offer three types of text alignment: Left, right and centered.

FAQ: LIGHT & TUBE COLORS

What is a tube color? Why does it matter?

Tube color refers to the color of PVC tubing that encases your sign’s LED bulbs. On top of your choice for the LED light color, different colored tubes can enhance the color vibrance of your sign. When using a plain white tube, the color of your led light stays uninterfered and therefore results more brighter, pastel toned colors. Meanwhile, when using a colored tube, the colour from led light will penetrate through another coat of tube colour on top, resulting more solid and vivid colors.

FAQ: BACKDROP SHAPES & MATERIALS

What’s a backdrop?

The backdrop refers to the back panel that holds all LED neon pieces together. For Indoor use, we offer clear acrylic backdrops with four shape options available: Square, rectangle, circle and outlined. For Outdoor use, we offer waterproof metal plates with three different shapes to choose from: Square, rectangle, and circle.

Where can I place my sign?

Indoor signs are meant to for indoor placement and outdoor signs are meant for placement outdoors.

Why are outdoor signs more expensive?

We use a waterproof metal for the backdrops of outdoor signs instead of clear acrylic Plexiglas. We charge an additional 30% to cover the cost of this metal.

FAQ: INSTALLATION METHODS

What will be included with my neon sign?

Installation materials such as nails and plastic plugs are included with all neon signs. Also included is an adaptor that can be plugged into any North American Standard outlet (120V).

What is not included with my neon sign?

Hardware tools required for installation such as drills will not be included in your neon sign.

How do I install my neon sign?

Visit our Installation page for detailed instructions on how to install your neon sign.

Is there a warranty included?

All neon signs include a one-year warranty that covers electrical and external components.
